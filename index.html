Simon Benigeri

I am a PhD student in Artificial Intelligence at Northwestern University, advised by Professor Larry Birnbaum. My primary research areas are Natural Language Processing and Conversational AI. Our goal is to build practical conversational AI systems and we hope to do this by making LLMs more reliable and robust to tasks, domains, and conversational behaviors. I recently interned at Samsung Research America, AI Center on the Language & Personal Intelligence Team, where I developed a novel method to improve the reasoning capabilities of small LLMs.
